Spicy Miso Ramen

About this dish

This miso ramen ramen recipe flavor originates from Hokkaido Japan, is flavored with soybean paste (miso) to create a rich and complex broth. Our recipe uses both white miso and red miso; the darker the color, the longer the soybeans have fermented (and richer the flavor).

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Nori seaweed
  • white miso paste
  • Chilli sesame oil
  • Sliced mushrooms

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together miso, garlic, and 3 Tbsp. of water.
  2. If adding spinach or mushrooms, sautee during this step and keep warm.
  3. Fill a medium pot 3/4 full of water, cover, and bring to a boil.
  4. In a separate large stock pot, warm half of the sesame oil on medium heat. Add the miso paste from step 1 and saute for 2-3 minutes.
    5. Add the ramen noodles to the medium pot of boiling water from step 3 and cook for 3-4 minutes, then drain.
  5. Divide cooked noodles between large soup bowls, ladle the miso broth over the noodles, and add your favorite toppings: menma (seasoned bamboo), green onions, nori (seaweed), sesame oil, and chili garlic sauce (spicy)!
